4. Booking Process

For Customers

  • You may browse available providers, view their profiles, services, and pricing, and submit a booking request.

  • You must provide accurate details about your location and the service you need so the provider can prepare appropriately.

For Providers

  • You may accept or decline any booking request.
  • Once you accept a booking, you are expected to complete the service as agreed.

5. Payment Terms

  • The customer is responsible for ensuring the correct payment amount is transferred.
  • FixIt will confirm receipt of payment and update the booking status accordingly.

6. Commission Structure

FixIt charges a 5% commission on all completed bookings, regardless of payment method. The commission is deducted from the provider's earnings at the time of withdrawal.

FixIt reserves the right to change the commission rate with reasonable advance notice to providers.

7. Withdrawals

  • Providers may request a withdrawal of their available earnings through the app.
  • Withdrawal requests are processed manually within 1–3 business days.
  • FixIt reserves the right to verify withdrawal requests before processing to prevent fraud.
  • A minimum withdrawal amount may apply.
  • Payouts are made to the provider's designated bank account (ABA, ACLEDA, or Wing).

8. Cancellation Policy

Before Acceptance

After Acceptance

  • If a customer cancels after a provider has accepted the booking, the cancellation is subject to the provider's discretion. The provider may choose to waive any charges or request reasonable compensation for time and preparation.
  • If a provider cancels an accepted booking, they should notify the customer as soon as possible. Repeated cancellations by a provider may affect their standing on the platform.

No-Shows

If a customer is not available at the agreed location and time without prior notice, the provider may mark the booking as a no-show.
