Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Effective April 2026

1. Introduction

FixIt ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

FixIt is a mobile marketplace connecting customers with AC service providers in Cambodia. By using FixIt, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use FixIt:

Account Information

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (if uploaded)
  • Account type (customer or provider)

Location Data

  • Your real-time location when using the app, used to find nearby providers and calculate distances.
  • The service address you provide when placing a booking.

Booking Data

  • Service requests and booking history
  • Messages between customers and providers
  • Payment confirmations and transaction records
  • Reviews and ratings

Device Information

  • Push notification tokens (used to send you booking updates and notifications)
  • Device type and operating system (collected automatically by the app platform)

Provider-Specific Data

  • Services offered and pricing
  • Service areas and availability
  • Earnings and withdrawal history

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Match customers with nearby providers based on location and service needs.
  • Process bookings — create, manage, and track service requests.
  • Process payments — record transactions, calculate commissions, and manage provider earnings and withdrawals.
  • Send push notifications — booking updates, payment confirmations, and important service alerts.
  • Enable communication between customers and providers for booking coordination.
  • Display reviews and ratings to help users make informed decisions.
  • Improve the platform — analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience.
  • Ensure safety and security — detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.

4. Third-Party Services

FixIt uses the following third-party services to operate the platform:

Supabase

  • Purpose: Database, user authentication, and backend infrastructure.
  • Data shared: All account data, booking data, and transaction records are stored in Supabase.
  • Security: Supabase provides row-level security (RLS) policies to ensure users can only access data they are authorized to see.
Supabase servers are located outside of Cambodia. By using FixIt, you consent to your data being transferred to and stored on servers outside the Kingdom of Cambodia.

Expo (Push Notifications)

  • Purpose: Delivering push notifications to your device.
  • Data shared: Push notification tokens and notification content (e.g., "Your booking has been accepted").

Google Maps

  • Purpose: Displaying maps, calculating distances, and providing location-based services within the app.
  • Data shared: Location coordinates for map display and distance calculations.
  • Privacy: Google Maps is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with row-level security (RLS) policies that restrict data access at the database level.
  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Profile photos and uploaded images are stored in secure cloud storage with access controls.
  • We regularly review our security practices, but no system is completely immune to risk. We encourage you to protect your account credentials.

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account remains active.
  • Closed accounts: If you close your account or request deletion, we will remove your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required for legal or regulatory compliance, financial record-keeping, or resolving disputes.
  • Booking and transaction records may be retained in anonymized or aggregated form for business analytics even after account deletion.

7. Your Rights

Access

You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you by contacting us.

Correction

You can update most of your account information directly in the app (name, phone, email, profile photo). For corrections to other data, contact us and we will update it promptly.

Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by contacting us at fixitkh26@gmail.com. Upon receiving a deletion request, we will remove your personal data except where retention is legally required. Please note that deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

Notification Preferences

You can manage push notification permissions through your device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

FixIt is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

If we become aware that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a minor has provided us with their data, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

FixIt operates in Cambodia, but our backend infrastructure (Supabase) is hosted on servers located outside of Cambodia. By using FixIt, you acknowledge and consent to your personal data being transferred to, stored, and processed in countries outside the Kingdom of Cambodia.

We ensure that our third-party service providers maintain appropriate security standards to protect your data regardless of where it is stored.

10. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements.
  • We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email.
  • The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy will be revised accordingly.
  • Continued use of FixIt after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
